# Product Requirements Document — AURA AI

## 1. Vision & Mission
*   **Vision:** A world where recruiting is defined by actual human competence, capabilities, and compatibility, rather than superficial matching of text strings on a resume.
*   **Mission:** Build an enterprise-grade AI Recruitment Intelligence Platform that enables recruiters to understand, evaluate, and source candidates using deep semantic representation, objective multi-dimensional feature scoring, and explainable decision paths.

---

## 2. Problem Statement
Traditional Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) rely on simple keyword matching. This triggers two major dysfunctions:
1.  **Keyword Stuffing:** Candidates inflate resumes with search words to beat the filter, degrading evaluation quality.
2.  **High False Negatives:** Exceptional candidates who describe their accomplishments in synonyms or natural phrasing instead of mechanical jargon are discarded.
3.  **Lack of Transparency:** Modern AI matching tools function as black boxes, failing to explain *why* a candidate was ranked highly or rejected, which creates compliance risks and trust issues.

---

## 3. Target Users & Personas
*   **Primary Persona: Elena Rostova, Senior Enterprise Recruiter**
    *   *Context:* Handles high-volume sourcing for technical roles. Under constant pressure to screen 500+ candidates per job description.
    *   *Friction:* Frustrated by unqualified candidates scoring high on keyword matches, and unable to explain candidate selections to engineering hiring managers.
    *   *Goal:* Quickly identify the top 5% of candidates based on real capabilities and explain the reasoning transparently.

---

## 4. Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
**"Hiring Beyond Keywords."**  
AURA AI combines deep semantic understanding (context-aware cosine similarity) with an objective, six-dimensional Feature scoring engine, coupled with an explainable Decision Intelligence layer that generates plain-text feedback and confidence ratings for recruiters.

---

## 5. Success Metrics
*   **Accuracy:** Match ranking correlation with hiring manager ratings > 0.85.
*   **Throughput:** Process and score a batch of 100,000 candidates under 2 seconds.
*   **Explainability:** 100% of generated ranking positions carry clear, structured reason codes.

---

## 6. Non-Goals
*   No automated interview scheduling or candidate messaging workflows.
*   No automated candidate rejection emails (the human recruiter remains the decision-maker).
*   No real-time audio/video screening capabilities.
